Webdev Resources

Below are some links to free resources that will help you start your journey.

  • W3Schools.com: a great free resource. Check out their tutorials and reference sections.
  • Visual Studio Code: a free text editor with lots of great extensions to help you learn web development. It's also one of the industry standards. Here's a tutorial on setting it up. Here's another.
  • freeCodeCamp: free to start. Project-based learning.
  • Traversy Media: a great YouTube channel with lots of beneficial tutorials. I'd recommend subscribing.
